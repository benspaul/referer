referer_data.csv contains data from a ficticious A/B test.
What can you tell me about the results?

DATA
referer - The source of the traffic. One of the following:
    "FB"
    "Google"
    "MSmag"
    "Other"
    "YouTube"
    "blogher"
    "caranddriver"
    "footballoutsider"
    "pioneeringwoman"

country - country of origin for traffic. One of the following:
    "AU"
    "CA"
    "Other"
    "UK"
    "US"

landing_page - Either a or b. Denotes the landing page being tested.

roi - The return on investment. i.e. The profit after expense.
